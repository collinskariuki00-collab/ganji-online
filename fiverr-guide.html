How to Start on Fiverr (Beginner Guide)

Fiverr is one of the easiest platforms to start making money online by selling services. In this guide, you will learn how to create gigs, attract clients, and earn your first money.

Step 1: Create Your Account

Go to Fiverr.com and sign up. Use your real name and a professional email.

Step 2: Set Up Your Profile

Add a clear profile picture, write a short bio, and list your skills. A strong profile helps you stand out.

Step 3: Create Your First Gig

Choose a service you can offer (e.g., graphic design, writing, video editing). Set a competitive price and write a clear description.

Step 4: Get Your First Order

Share your gig, use keywords, and be patient. Your first order is the hardest, but once you get it, more clients will follow.

Step 5: Deliver Quality Work

Always deliver high-quality work on time. Good reviews will increase your earnings.
